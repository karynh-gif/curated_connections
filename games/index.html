How to Play
1
The Judge draws a card with scenarios and secretly ranks them — least bad first, worst last.
2
Pass the phone. Every other player ranks all scenarios in the same order they think the Judge did. Try to think like them.
3
Score by position. Get position 4 right = 4 pts. Get position 6 right = 6 pts. Judge always scores equal to the highest player that round.
🎡
The Wheel spins every round — Face Value, Double Top, Judge Jackpot, and more. Auto-spins in 5 seconds or tap first.
↻
Decks rotate automatically — Really Bad Day → Survive This → Best Day Ever → Road Trip → Work Day → Family Reunion → That's My Life → Kid Logic.
