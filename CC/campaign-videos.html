Campaign 04
The Dinner Table
20 sec · Instagram · Emotional
Script
"Put your phone down."

"But it's asking me something
I've never been asked before."

Curated Connections — use it together.
HeyGen Prompt
Scene
Family dinner. Parent tells teenager to put their phone down. Teenager turns the screen toward the parent — it shows a Curated Connections card. Parent reads it. A beat. Then they actually answer. The whole table goes quiet and listens.
Tone
Warm, real, slightly emotional. Soft dialogue or text on screen. End on parent and teenager actually talking. Logo fades in.
Campaign 05
The Mom One
20 sec · Mother's Day · Emotional
Script
She's been sitting across from you
for 30 years.

You've never asked her that question.

Curated Connections — start the conversation.

Moms are special. Memories are too.
HeyGen Prompt
Scene
Adult child and mother at a kitchen table, soft afternoon light. Adult reads from phone. Pauses. Looks up at mom. Asks the question. Mom's face changes — surprised, then touched. She starts to answer.
Tone
Quiet, warm, emotional. Ambient kitchen sounds. Text overlays only. "Moms are special. Memories are too." as final line before logo.
