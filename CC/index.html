Questions

Good ones, obviously.

Do I need an account to play?

You can try a demo card without an account. To save progress, access Your Truth, or unlock full decks, you'll create a free account and choose a plan.

Can I play alone or only with a group?

Both. Solo play is powerful for reflection. The cards are designed to work whether you're by yourself or with 2–8 people. Phase 2 adds live multiplayer room codes for remote play.

What is Your Truth, exactly?

After you answer a card question, Your Truth uses AI to distill what you said into 1–2 sentences that feel unmistakably, specifically true about you — not generic affirmations, but a reflection of what you actually shared.

What is Living Legacy?

Living Legacy is your personal archive. It saves selected answers, your Your Truth distillations, and over time builds a record of your stories and discoveries in one place. Available on the Deep Connect plan.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Cancel any time from your account dashboard. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
