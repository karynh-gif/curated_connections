How to Play
1
A card is drawn with 6 terrible scenarios. The Active Player secretly ranks them from least terrible to absolutely worst.
2
The phone is passed. Each other player secretly predicts which scenario the active player ranked as their WORST.
3
Everyone reveals together. Correct predictions earn 2 points. The active player earns 1 point for each person who guessed wrong — they know you well!
+
A conversation prompt follows every reveal — the real game is the discussion.
