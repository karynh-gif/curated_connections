What it is
Not a card.
Not a scrapbook.
A living record.
A time capsule collects memories, reflections, and moments from everyone who matters — while those moments are still fresh. The graduate receives it when the time is right. Their own words from right now. The things their people noticed that they'll forget they said.
Some people start one the summer before senior year. Some start the day before graduation. Both are right.
How it works
Simple.
Meaningful.
1
Start a capsule for them
Enter their name and what you're celebrating. Pick graduation 2026. You're the Keeper — you control everything until you're ready to present it.
2
Invite the people who know them
Share a link with their coach, their teammates, their teachers, their family. Each person adds their own memory. No account needed. Takes five minutes.
3
Answer prompts about them
We send you weekly prompts — questions that capture who they are right now. What you see in them. What you want them to carry forward. Your answers, their words, all in one place.

Present it when the moment is right
At graduation. At dinner. On a quiet morning before everything changes. One link. Every voice. Everything they meant to people, collected in one place forever.
